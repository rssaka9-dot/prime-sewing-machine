# Prime Sewing Machine - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Mission
**"Being the best sewing machine seller"**

---

## Brand Overview
Prime Sewing Machine is a premium sewing machine retailer located in Kiyembe City Complex, Kampala, Uganda. We specialize in high-quality sewing machines with expert guidance, training, repair services, and comprehensive warranty support.

---

## Logo Design

### Logo Elements
- **Sewing machine silhouette** (modern, minimalist design)
- **Needle and thread** forming a subtle "P" shape
- **Company name:** "PRIME" in bold, modern font
- **Tagline:** "Premium Quality • Expert Guidance"
- **File location:** `/public/logo.svg`

### Logo Variations
- **Horizontal version:** Used on desktop/web
- **Stacked version:** Used on mobile/narrow screens
- **Icon version:** Standalone sewing machine icon (32x32, 64x64, 128x128px)

### Logo Usage Rules
- ✅ Minimum size: 100px width
- ✅ Clear space around logo: 20px minimum on all sides
- ✅ Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- ✅ Use on light backgrounds (white, light gray)
- ✅ Can use white version on dark backgrounds

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headlines)
**Font Name:** Poppins Bold / Montserrat Bold
- **Type:** Modern sans-serif
- **Weight:** Bold (700)
- **Usage:** Logo, main headings (H1), CTAs, buttons, hero text
- **Size Range:** 28px - 48px (desktop), 20px - 32px (mobile)
- **Example:** "PRIME SEWING MACHINE"

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Font Name:** Inter / Roboto
- **Type:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Weight:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600)
- **Usage:** Body text, product descriptions, navigation, paragraphs
- **Size Range:** 14px - 18px (desktop), 12px - 16px (mobile)
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for readability

### Font Fallbacks
```
Headlines: 'Poppins', 'Montserrat', Arial, sans-serif;
Body: 'Inter', 'Roboto', Arial, sans-serif;
```

## Visual Style

### Design Principles
1. **Modern Minimalist** - Clean, uncluttered, professional
2. **Geometric Precision** - Sharp lines, balanced spacing
3. **Premium Feel** - High-quality imagery, sophisticated layouts
4. **User-Centric** - Clear navigation, intuitive design
5. **Trust & Reliability** - Consistent, professional presentation

### Imagery Guidelines
- ✅ High-quality, professional product photography
- ✅ Well-lit sewing machines with clear details
- ✅ Lifestyle images showing machines in use
- ✅ Customer testimonial photos (professional headshots)
- ✅ Warm, inviting lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- ✅ Consistent background styles (white, light gray, or contextual)
- ✅ Show different machine angles and details

### Spacing & Layout
- **Grid system:** 12-column grid (desktop), 4-column (mobile)
- **Padding:** 16px base unit (16px, 32px, 48px, 64px)
- **Margins:** Consistent spacing between sections
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned text, centered CTAs/images

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover states:** Gold (#D4AF37) highlights or subtle blue shadow
- **Active states:** Gold background with white text
- **Focus states:** Blue outline (accessibility)
- **Transitions:** Smooth 0.3s ease transitions

## Accessibility & Inclusivity
- ✅ High contrast ratios (WCAG AA standard)
- ✅ Descriptive alt text for all images
- ✅ Keyboard navigation support
- ✅ Mobile responsive design
- ✅ Clear, readable fonts
- ✅ Sufficient whitespace
- ✅ Color not sole information indicator

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO
- Use the complete color palette consistently
- Maintain proper spacing and alignment
- Use high-quality, professional images
- Keep designs clean and uncluttered
- Ensure all text is readable
- Use the specified fonts
- Maintain brand professionalism

### ❌ DON'T
- Use outdated or pixelated images
- Mix fonts excessively
- Apply filters that distort colors
- Use low-contrast text
- Overcrowd layouts
- Use watermarked or stock photos (when possible)
- Deviate significantly from brand colors
